About Our Company

Deyanj Nigeria limited is a dynamic and innovative agro commodity trading company, dedicated to bridging the gap between Nigeria's agricultural potential and global market demands. With a strong focus on quality, efficiency, and sustainability, we specialize in sourcing, processing, and exporting high-quality agricultural products, including grains, roots and tubers, legumes, oilseeds and spices. At the heart of Deyanj Nigeria Limited operations lies in our expertise in procuring high-quality food produce directly from farmers and supplying to local off takers who are aimed at processing produce into finished goods. By establishing strong partnerships with farmers across the country, Deyanj Nigeria Limited ensures a consistent supply of fresh and seasonal produce, through a meticulous and transparent\ procurement process, we maintain strict quality control standards to ensure that only the finest produce reaches our customers.

Deyanj Nigeria Limited has established a sizeable presence in the agro business, succesfully completing imports and exports on multiple varieties of agrobased products.
Our global footprint extends to multiple product categories across agricultural commodities, processed Agri products, Agri Inputs, Industrial Chemicals and food products.
Our global footprint enables us to leverage our sourcing networks and provide customers with high-quality products that meet their specific needs.
We work closely with all our stakeholders to ensure that our value chain is sustainable, and our carbon footprint minimal.
We are deeply committed to the welfare of our people and the communities where we work.

  • Vision: Transforming the agricultural landscape through cutting-edge technology, environmentally conscious methods, and customer-centric approach, redefining the future of food production and distribution.

  • Mission: To source, process, and deliver high-quality agro commodities, exceeding customer expectations while promoting sustainable agriculture, fair trade practices, and community development.

  • Core Values: Sustainability, Integrity, Customer-centricity, Innovation, Community engagement, Transparency, Accountability, Quality, Fair trade, Environmental stewardship. Deyanj's value chain is built on excellence in sourcing, processing, and branding. Deyanj has developed sourcing networks directly with farm-gate suppliers and manufacturing processors, allowing the company to establish a strong presence in its core areas of operations across Africa . Deyanj emphasizes sustainability and ethical business practices in its sourcing strategy, while its branding strategy focuses on building strong,trustworthy brands that promote sustainable agriculture. By establishing a strong presence across the value chain, Deyanj delivers excellence at every step to meet the needs of its customers around the world.
